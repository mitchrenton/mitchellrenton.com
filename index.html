A little bit about me

I enjoy a great work-life balance which ensures I don’t lose focus on what’s important. Whether it’s keeping up to date with web technology, making time for friends & family or keeping a project intuitive and productive.

I’m lucky enough to be doing a job that I love. I started tinkering with websites after university. Curious about how sites were built I would look at the source code and figure out how things were rendered.

Soon after I got a copy of Photoshop and started designing sites. I now lead a team of multi-disciplined UX professionals at Adecco.

Here's what I do

  • I research, analyse, ask questions
  • I sketch things on paper, post-its, mole-skins
  • I wireframe with pencils, markers, Axure, Photoshop
  • I prototype using Foundation or InVision
  • I test with Morae
  • I iterate
  • I design with Photoshop or in the browser
  • I build using Yoeman, Grunt and Bower
  • I write scalable accessible markup
  • I analyse
  • I repeat
  • But most importantly...
  • I trust my instincts
